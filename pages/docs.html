Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my projects?
You can save your projects in two ways: locally to your browser's storage, or to Google Drive for cloud backup. Click the save icon in the file explorer and choose your preferred method. Google Drive integration requires you to sign in with your Google account.
What is the demo mode limit?
Demo mode allows you to try the platform for 3 free attempts. Each attempt includes 30 minutes of usage. After that, you'll need to create an account to continue using all features.
Can I use my own API keys?
Yes, in the settings page you can configure your own API keys for various services including AI models, Google Drive, and custom endpoints. This gives you more control and higher usage limits.
How do I collaborate with others?
You can share your projects by saving them to Google Drive and sharing the link. We're also working on real-time collaboration features that will be available in the next update.
